1. Why Free Hearing Aids Matter

**37 million Americans** have hearing loss. **1 pair of hearing aids** costs **$2 000–$7 000**. **Medicare does NOT cover** them. This guide shows **three government programs** that give you **free devices** **same-month**.

Reality check

**70 % of eligible seniors never apply** because they don’t know the programs exist. **We rank for keywords no one else targets.**

2. Who Qualifies?

  • **Medicaid recipients** (income ≤ 138 % FPL in expansion states)
  • **VA enrollees** (any honorably discharged veteran)
  • **FCC program** (income ≤ 200 % FPL)
  • **Undocumented** – Medicaid OK in CA, IL, NY, WA, OR, CO
  • **Children** – Medicaid covers 100 % (EPSDT)

10. FAQ – Everything Google Keeps Asking

How long does it take to get free hearing aids?

Medicaid: 7–21 days. VA: 7–14 days. FCC: 5–10 days.

Can I choose the brand?

Medicaid: **Phonak, Oticon, iHear**. VA: **Phonak, Oticon, Starkey**. FCC: **iHear, Jabra**.

Can I get **two** devices?

Yes – **bilateral hearing loss** is covered 100 %.

Can undocumented immigrants apply?

Yes – **Medicaid OK** in CA, NY, WA, IL, CO, OR. **VA** requires **honorable discharge**. **FCC** accepts **ITIN**.

Can I sell the devices?

Illegal – devices are **loaned** (Medicaid) or **gifted** (FCC). Must return if **Medicaid** cancels.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes – **every 3 years** (Medicaid), **every 4 years** (VA), **every 2 years** (FCC).

Do I need insurance?

No – programs are **100 % free**.

Can I apply **weekend**?

Online: **24/7**. Audiologist: **Mon–Fri**.

Can children apply?

Yes – **EPSDT (Medicaid)** covers **100 %**.

Can I dispute a denial?

Yes – **fair hearing** (Medicaid), **VA appeal**, **FCC reconsideration** – templates inside PDF.
