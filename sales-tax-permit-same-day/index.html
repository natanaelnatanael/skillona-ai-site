4. Step-by-Step Universal Process

  1. Get EIN (free at irs.gov/online-ein) – 5 min
  2. Choose state dashboard (links in table)
  3. Fill application – business name, address, NAICS code, product type
  4. Receive permit number instantly – download PDF, print, frame

5. EIN Checklist – Do This First (5 min)

Before you start:
  • Legal business name (or your full name for sole prop)
  • SSN or ITIN (foreign sellers use **ITIN**)
  • Business address (can be home or virtual mailbox)
  • NAICS code (find inside PDF – e-commerce = 454110)
  • Expected monthly sales (estimate is OK)

Apply: irs.gov/online-ein – approval **instant**, PDF download immediately.

6. Foreign Sellers (No SSN) – Same-Day Route

  • Apply for **ITIN** (Form W-7) – **or** use **EIN only** (allowed in 40 states)
  • Use **virtual mailbox** (Anytime Mailbox affiliate – $10/month)
  • Select **“marketplace facilitator”** if selling only on Amazon/Shopify – **no permit needed in most states**
  • Still get permit if you **sell on your own site** or **Etsy**

7. Marketplace Facilitator Exception – When You DON’T Need a Permit

If you **only** sell through **Amazon, Etsy, Walmart, eBay, Shopify Payments**, the **platform** collects and remits tax for you. **No permit required** in **CA, NY, TX, FL, IL, PA, OH, GA, NC, MI, etc.**

Important

If you **also sell on your own site (Shopify store, WooCommerce, craft fair)**, you **must** get a permit. This guide shows **how to get it same-day**.

Most states: **$0**. FL: $5. AZ: $12. WI: $20. NJ: $50 (refundable).

Yes – **nexus = permit**. Use **SSUTA states** (24) if you want **one filing**.
